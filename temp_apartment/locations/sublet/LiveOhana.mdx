## What it is and how it works

A sublet marketplace positioning itself as Seattle's largest sublet and sublease community,
advertising furnished month-to-month options. On paper the pitch matches Tom's need almost
exactly: furnished, one-month minimum, whole-unit sublets.

**What is unverified — and it is nearly everything that matters:**

* **Listing counts** — unknown
* **Whole-unit vs. room ratio** — unknown. This is the critical one. If it is room-dominant
  like most sublet channels, it is useless for Tom regardless of volume.
* **Eastside coverage** — unknown. It is a Seattle-metro site, and the ~20 minute drive
  figure suggests Seattle proper, not Redmond.

The site is **JavaScript-rendered**, meaning its listings do not appear in a plain page fetch.
None of the above could be established during this research. The "$1,200–3,000" range in the
CSV is an **estimate**, not a quote.

## How to search it effectively

```
https://liveohana.ai/sublet/seattle
```

Open it in a real browser — a plain fetch returns nothing useful. Then answer three questions
in this order, and stop as soon as one fails:

1. **Are there whole units, or only rooms?** Rooms disqualify it immediately.
2. **Is there anything in Redmond, East Bellevue, or South Kirkland?** Seattle-proper
   listings are a 20+ minute drive and miss the target.
3. **Are the month-to-month claims real per-listing, or just site-level marketing?**

If it clears all three, it becomes interesting. If it fails any, close the tab. **Budget ten
minutes to find out — not an hour.**

## Scam risk

**Unknown, which is itself the finding.** No vetting process, escrow arrangement, or landlord
verification could be confirmed. An unverified marketplace should be assumed to carry the
same fraud exposure as [Craigslist](./Craigslist_Eastside.mdx) until proven otherwise — the
default state of a sublet listing board is unvetted.

Apply the standard rules without exception:

* **Never send money before physically walking the unit and meeting the person.**
* No wire, Zelle, Venmo, Cash App, or gift cards — none are reversible.
* Treat any furnished Eastside 1BR under ~$1,800 as presumptively fraudulent. The bottom of
  this site's estimated range ($1,200) sits squarely in that zone.
* If it is a sublet, **get the landlord's written consent** — an unauthorized sublet can get
  Tom evicted through no fault of his own.
