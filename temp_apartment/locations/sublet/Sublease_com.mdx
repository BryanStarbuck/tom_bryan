## What it is and how it works

Despite the name, this is **not a residential sublet marketplace**. Searches for Seattle-area
residential sublets surface **commercial office sublease brokers** instead — companies
brokering unused corporate office space, not apartments.

That is a completely different industry. It has nothing to offer a retiree looking for a
1–2 month place to live.

## How to search it effectively

You don't. There is no useful search path.

```
https://www.sublease.com/
```

The name is the only reason this site entered the search at all. **Do not confuse it with
[Sublet.com](./Sublet_com.mdx)**, which is a real (if troubled) residential marketplace with
actual Redmond listings.

## Scam risk

Not applicable — there is nothing here to transact with. The only cost this site can impose
is wasted time.
