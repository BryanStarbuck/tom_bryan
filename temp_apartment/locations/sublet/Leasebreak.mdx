## What it is and how it works

Leasebreak is a genuine, functioning lease-break and short-term sublet marketplace — for
**whole units**, which is the right product. The concept is a good fit for Tom's need: people
leaving a lease early, looking for someone to take over a partial term.

The problem is purely geographic. It serves the **five boroughs of NYC and New Jersey**. It
does not operate in Washington State at all. There is no Redmond, Bellevue, or Kirkland
inventory to find — not thin inventory, **none**.
