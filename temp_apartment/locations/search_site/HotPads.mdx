## What it is and how it works

A Zillow-owned rental site. Because it is Zillow-owned, **its inventory duplicates Zillow's** —
if you have worked Zillow thoroughly, HotPads adds little new stock.

What it adds is a **different filter set**. HotPads exposes a **for-rent-by-owner (FRBO)**
filter that Zillow does not surface as cleanly, plus a furnished filter and a sublets filter.
That is the entire reason to open this site.

**Why FRBO is the point.** The [overview](../../overview.mdx) established that no conventional
managed building on the Eastside writes true month-to-month. The people who will are **private
owners** — someone with one condo, between tenants, who would rather have two months of rent
than zero. **No site has a "will you do two months?" filter.** FRBO is the closest available
proxy, because it isolates the population of landlords who *can* say yes without asking a
corporate policy.

**Bellevue: 98 for-rent-by-owner, 147 furnished.** That is a real, workable call list.

## How to search it effectively

The FRBO surface — this is the URL that matters:

```
https://hotpads.com/bellevue-wa/for-rent-by-owner
```

Swap the city for the other targets:

```
https://hotpads.com/redmond-wa/for-rent-by-owner
https://hotpads.com/kirkland-wa/for-rent-by-owner
```

Also useful: the **furnished** filter (147 in Bellevue) and the **sublets** filter.

**Avoid its Rooms section entirely.** Same rule as
[Craigslist's `est/roo`](../sublet/Craigslist_Eastside.mdx) — rooms mean roommates, which the
charter rules out.

## Does the filter actually work?

**There is no filter to work — and that was verified, not assumed.**

The filter panel contains: **beds, baths, price, sqft, pets, furnished, and sublets**. There
is **no lease-length field**. And `?lease=month` **is not a real parameter** — appending it
does nothing. Do not build a URL around it and do not assume a hidden filter exists.

So the honest framing of this site:

| | |
|---|---|
| **Month-to-month filter** | Does not exist. Verified. |
| **Inventory** | Duplicates [Zillow](./Zillow.mdx) — nothing new |
| **Actual value** | The **FRBO filter** — the private-landlord population |

**The FRBO filter does work**, and it is a genuinely different cut of the market than
[Zumper](./Zumper.mdx) or [Apartments.com](./Apartments_com.mdx) provide — both of those skew
to managed buildings, which is the inventory least able to help Tom.

The trade: you get the right *population* of landlords but must ask every one of them the term
question by phone, because the site cannot filter it. That is still a better use of an hour
than a filter that silently lies, which is what [Zillow](./Zillow.mdx) offers.
