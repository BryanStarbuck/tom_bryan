## Why this is (or isn't) a fit

**The term is the crux, and here it is genuinely unresolved — which is why this building is worth a call rather than a skip.** The published term is 12 months. If that's the real answer, this is not a fit: **12 × $3,435 = $41,220 for a 2-month need**, which is absurd, and the page ends there. But AvalonBay states that its **Furnished+ program supports 30-day terms at select communities**. If Esterra Park is one of them, this becomes one of the best options in the entire search. **That single question decides everything. Ask it first.**

**On budget, it's the closest near-miss in Redmond.** $3,435–3,485 is $135–185 over $3,300. That is a rounding error compared to every other furnished option — [Blueground's Redmond inventory](../blueground/Blueground.mdx) is estimated at $3,500–5,000, [Avalon at Bear Creek's](./Avalon_at_Bear_Creek.mdx) Furnished+ 1BR is $3,960, and [Eaves Redmond Campus](./Eaves_Redmond_Campus.mdx) is $3,510. Esterra Park is the cheapest turnkey furnished door in Redmond that we found.

**And the price is genuinely all-in, which matters more than it looks.** Utilities and WiFi are included. That means the number above is not a base rent waiting to be inflated — there's no furniture rental to add, no CORT delivery, no electric account to open and close, no internet install for a 60-day stay. Compare that to [The Luke](./The_Luke.mdx) at $2,050: after premium, utilities, and furniture, The Luke lands around $2,800–3,200 — which is *within $300 of Esterra Park* and comes with far more logistics. **Esterra Park's $185 of overage buys away the entire unfurnished trap.** That's a good trade if the term works.

**It's also a Blueground building.** Blueground runs furnished 30-night-minimum inventory at Esterra Park. That is a **second, independent path into the same building** — if AvalonBay says 12 months only, [call Blueground](../blueground/Blueground.mdx) and ask about Esterra Park units directly. Blueground has no published prices and its dynamic pricing punishes short stays (estimated $3,500–5,000/mo), so it will likely be more expensive than the direct rate — but it may be the only way to get a 30-day term in this building.

**Privacy and distance are clean.** Private apartment, no roommates, Overlake in Redmond, zero drive.

If Furnished+ prices a 30-day stay at the same $3,435, this is a $185/month overage and an easy yes. If they add $600, it's $4,035 and it's out. **Get the number.**

If the term is truly 12 months only: **12 × $3,435 = $41,220.** Not a fit. Move to [Avalon at Bear Creek](./Avalon_at_Bear_Creek.mdx), which will write 2 months.

## When you call

Call **206-339-6837**.

1. **Does Furnished+ at Esterra Park support a 30-day term?** This is the whole page. AvalonBay says select communities do — is this one? If the answer is 12 months only, thank them and hang up.
2. **If yes: what is the exact rate for a 30-day and a 60-day stay?** Is there a short-term premium on top of $3,435, and how much?
3. Confirm exactly what "utilities and WiFi included" covers — electric, water, sewer, trash, internet?
4. Is parking included in that rate, or extra?
5. What does the Furnished+ package actually contain? Kitchenware and linens, or just furniture?
6. What's available on Tom's move-in date? Studio or 1BR?
7. **Income requirement, and asset-based qualification for a retiree on a fixed income?**
8. Elevator, ground-floor unit, accessibility features?

**If AvalonBay says no to 30 days:** call [Blueground](../blueground/Blueground.mdx) at 1-917-259-1222 and ask specifically about Avalon Esterra Park units. Same building, 30-day minimum, different (and probably higher) price.
