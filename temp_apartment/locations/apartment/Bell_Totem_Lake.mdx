## Why this is (or isn't) a fit

**The term fails on arithmetic.** 6-month floor, confirmed 6–15 months. **6 × $1,865 =
$11,190 for a 2-month need** — roughly **$7,460 for months Tom does not use.** That ends it
as a plan.

**The reason to know about it anyway is accessibility.** Bell Totem Lake explicitly lists
**accessible units** — one of only a handful in the entire search, alongside
[Westwater](./Westwater.mdx), [The 101](./The_101_Kirkland.mdx), and
[Bellevue Ridge](./Bellevue_Ridge.mdx)'s ADA studio. For a senior, an accessible unit is not
an amenity; it is the requirement that silently eliminates most of the list. If Tom's needs
turn out to include one, the shortlist collapses to those four and this page re-enters the
conversation despite everything below.

**Geography is the second strike, and it is a real one.** Totem Lake / Finn Hill is **North
Kirkland** — outside the preferred (Redmond, East Bellevue) and acceptable (South Kirkland)
zones entirely. At ~18 minutes, this is **the farthest mainstream option in the search and
sits right at the edge of the 20-minute rule.** Every trip toward Redmond over 60 days pays
that tax. There is no reason to be up here unless something specific pulls you — and
accessibility is the only thing that does.

**Budget is fine.** A studio from $1,865 leaves ~$1,435/month of headroom. Money was never
the problem; the term and the map are.

shorter than 12 months often include additional fees," with no amount.

## When you call

Only call **425-217-3738** if accessibility is a hard requirement and the closer accessible
options have failed.

1. **"What does 'accessible unit' mean here — specifically?"** Grab bars? Roll-in shower?
   Wider doors? Elevator? Ground floor? This is the only reason to be on this call.
2. **"Is an accessible unit among the 11 available, and on what date?"**
3. **"Will you write anything shorter than 6 months?"** Ask once.
4. **"What is the short-term premium in dollars per month?"**
5. **"Which utilities are included? Is parking included?"**
6. **"What's the income requirement, and do you qualify on assets?"** Not published — a real
   risk for a retiree.
