## Why this is (or isn't) a fit

**The headline is a trap, and it is worth naming precisely.** Aspen Creek's listing shows a
1-month term, which is exactly what Tom needs and would make this a top-five option. It is
**renewals only**. That means an existing tenant, at the end of an existing lease, can go
month-to-month. Tom is not an existing tenant. **For a first-time move-in the term is 6–24
months, full stop.** The 1-month figure is real and it is completely useless to us.

Once you strip that away, the arithmetic is the same as every other 6-month building:
**6 × $2,349 = $14,094 for a 2-month need** — about **$9,400 paid for months Tom does not
use.** That is a fail, and no other feature on the page changes it.

**Watch for this same pattern elsewhere.** When a building advertises month-to-month, the
first question is always "from day one, or only on renewal?" It is the exact question to put
to [Bellevue Ridge](./Bellevue_Ridge.mdx), whose MTM flat fee looks like a real product but
should still be verified for new move-ins.

**Geography is the second problem.** Juanita / Totem Lake edge is **North Kirkland** — not
South Kirkland, not East Bellevue, not Redmond. It is outside all three target areas.
~17 minutes to Redmond is technically inside the 20-minute rule but near its edge, and it is
the wrong direction entirely. The building rates 4.0/5, which is fine, and parking is
included, which is nice. Neither is worth $9,400.

**The income requirement is 3× rent** — on $2,349 that is **$7,047/month**, a wall for a
retiree. Another reason not to spend time here.

## When you call

**Don't.** There is no question here whose answer changes the outcome.

The one exception: if Tom's timeline has genuinely stretched to six months or more, Aspen
Creek becomes an ordinary Kirkland apartment at an ordinary price, and the 1-month renewal
option would then let him go month-to-month at the end without signing another year. That is
a real feature — for a different problem than the one on this page. If that is the situation,
call **206-258-7540** and ask:

1. **"Confirm: is the 1-month term available at renewal only, or from move-in?"**
2. **"What is the shortest lease for a new resident?"**
3. **"Income is 3× rent — Tom is retired. Do you qualify on assets?"**
