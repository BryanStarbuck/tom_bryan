## Why this is (or isn't) a fit

**The term is the crux, and Bear Creek passes.** 2–12 month terms. It is one of only three Redmond buildings — with [The Luke](./The_Luke.mdx) and [Redmond Place](./Redmond_Place.mdx) — whose published floor matches Tom's actual 1–2 month need. Nobody else in Redmond gets there.

**What makes this building different is that it offers two paths, and they price very differently.**

*Path 1 — unfurnished at $2,680/month.* That's $620/month under the ceiling. But it's an empty apartment, so add the short-term premium, utilities, and furniture rental. See the math below.

*Path 2 — Furnished+ at $3,960/month, utilities and WiFi included.* Turnkey. Nothing to rent, nothing to set up, no utility accounts. But it's **$660/month over the $3,300 budget** — that's the honest number. If budget is firm, this path is out. If Tom would pay a premium to skip all the furniture logistics, it's worth pricing against the extended-stay hotels, which do the same job cheaper.

**Compare Furnished+ against its siblings before committing.** [Avalon Esterra Park](./Avalon_Esterra_Park.mdx) runs the same AvalonBay Furnished+ program at **$3,435–3,485** — only $135–185 over budget, and materially cheaper than Bear Creek's $3,960. [Eaves Redmond Campus](./Eaves_Redmond_Campus.mdx) is the third Furnished+ property at $3,510. If the furnished path is what Tom wants, **Esterra Park is the cheaper call.** Bear Creek's advantage over both is the 2-month term.

**Ask about the concession.** There's a published **one month free if leased by 7/25**. Concessions are almost always written for 12-month leases. **Ask explicitly whether it applies to a 2-month term** — if somehow it does, the math changes dramatically. Expect it doesn't.

**Location note:** Bear Creek is on the eastern edge of Redmond, not downtown. Still 0 minutes to Redmond, but it's a more suburban, car-dependent setting than [The Luke](./The_Luke.mdx) or [Redmond Place](./Redmond_Place.mdx) downtown.

## The real cost for 1-2 months

**Path 1 — unfurnished 1BR at $2,680, 2-month stay:**

| Line item | Monthly | 2 months |
|---|---|---|
| Base rent (1BR) | $2,680 | $5,360 |
| Short-term premium — **ESTIMATE, not a quote** | $300–600 | $600–1,200 |
| Utilities (not included on unfurnished) | ~$150–250 | $300–500 |
| Furniture rental — [CORT](../corporate_housing/CORT_Furniture_Rental.mdx) | $300+ | $600+ |
| **Estimated all-in** | **~$3,430–3,830/mo** | **~$6,860–7,660** |

**The unfurnished path breaks budget once you finish the math.** $2,680 looks like headroom; it isn't, because there are no studios here and the 1BR floor is $430 higher than [The Luke](./The_Luke.mdx)'s studio.

**Path 2 — Furnished+ 1BR at $3,960, utilities and WiFi included:**

| Line item | Monthly | 2 months |
|---|---|---|
| Furnished+ 1BR (all-in) | $3,960 | $7,920 |
| Short-term premium — **ESTIMATE, unpublished** | $0–600 | $0–1,200 |
| **Estimated all-in** | **$3,960+/mo** | **$7,920+** |

**$660/month over budget, before any short-term premium.** Clean, simple, and too expensive.

**Why the unfurnished trap matters here.** [CORT's Redmond showroom](../corporate_housing/CORT_Furniture_Rental.mdx) (2118 148th Ave NE, 425-242-5435) quotes $240–295/month for a 1BR package — **that's the 12-month rate**; 1–2 months prices higher, plus delivery, pickup, two moves, and utility account setup for a 60-day stay. That whole stack of cost and hassle is why [Extended Stay America Seattle-Redmond](../hotel/Extended_Stay_America_Seattle_Redmond.mdx) and [WoodSpring Suites Redmond](../hotel/WoodSpring_Suites_Seattle_Redmond.mdx) frequently beat an apartment at this duration — furnished, full kitchen, no lease, no utilities to open and close.

## When you call

Call **425-414-7427**.

1. **What is the exact short-term premium on a 2-month lease** — on the unfurnished unit, and on Furnished+?
2. **Does the "one month free by 7/25" concession apply to a 2-month term?** Almost certainly not, but ask.
3. Confirm the Furnished+ 1BR price and exactly what "utilities and WiFi included" covers.
4. **Does Furnished+ here support a 30-day term?** AvalonBay says select communities do — find out if this is one.
5. What's available on Tom's move-in date? Window is Jul 16 – Sep 27, 2026.
6. **Income requirement, and asset-based qualification for a retiree on fixed income?**
7. Elevator, ground-floor availability, garage vs. carport cost, pet fee.
