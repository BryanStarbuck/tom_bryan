## Why this is (or isn't) a fit

**The term is the crux and it fails.** The property's own page shows **12 months**.
**12 × $2,210 = $26,520 for a 2-month need.** A 12-month-only building is not a fit for a
60-day stay — that is the whole verdict, and the page could stop here.

**The one ambiguity is worth naming, and then dismissing.** An ApartmentList short-term
roundup describes Windsor Juanita Bay as having "variable lease lengths." The property page
says 12 months. When an aggregator and the property disagree, **believe the property** —
aggregator short-term categorization is marketing, not data. The same roundups list
buildings with 12- and 13-month terms on their "short-term" pages. This is a thin thread and
it is not worth pulling.

**Geography compounds it.** Juanita is **North Kirkland** — not South Kirkland, not East
Bellevue, not Redmond. It is outside every target area. ~18 minutes to Redmond is technically
inside the 20-minute rule but at its edge, and in the wrong direction. There is no reason to
be here rather than [South Kirkland](./Kirkland_Crossing.mdx) or
[Bridle Trails](./Modera_Bridle_Trails.mdx), both of which are closer to Redmond *and* in
better-preferred zones.

**Utilities are explicitly not included**, which is unusual to state so plainly and means
account setup and teardown on top of everything else for a stay Tom cannot legally shorten
below a year anyway.

| **Est. total for the 12-month minimum** | **~$30,000+** |

Against [Larkspur Landing at ~$5,100 for two months with no lease](../hotel/Larkspur_Landing_Bellevue.mdx),
this is roughly six times the cost to solve the same problem. There is no negotiation that
closes that gap.

**Short-term premiums are never published in this market** — every listing says only "leases
shorter than 12 months often include additional fees." Here there is no short term to attach
a premium to.

## When you call

**Don't.** The 12-month term is published by the property itself. If you want to spend two
minutes disproving the ApartmentList "variable" claim, call **425-546-0618** and ask one
question — *"What is the shortest lease you will write?"* — but expect "12 months," and plan
accordingly.
