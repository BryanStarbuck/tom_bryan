## Why this isn't a fit

**The company no longer exists.** On **2025-11-09, Marriott terminated Sonder's license.** The next
day, **2025-11-10**, Sonder announced an **immediate wind-down of operations and Chapter 7
liquidation**. Chapter 7 is not reorganization — it is liquidation. There is no service to buy. More
than **$200M was outstanding on a 2021 note.**

**What happened to guests is the reason this page exists.** When the wind-down hit, **guests were
locked out mid-stay and told to vacate within hours** — people who had paid for a place to sleep
found their key cards dead and were given no meaningful notice.

**That is exactly the failure mode this entire search exists to protect Tom from.** A 78-year-old,
alone, in an unfamiliar city, with his belongings inside an apartment he can no longer enter and no
one answering the phone. Not a bad review — a catastrophe. It is the single clearest argument in this
whole search for preferring operators with a phone number, a balance sheet, and a human at a desk.

**Do not contact them. Do not book anything presenting itself as Sonder.** Any site still taking
Sonder bookings should be treated as a scam surface, the same as
[Zeus Living](./Zeus_Living.mdx).
