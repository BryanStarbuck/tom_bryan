## Why this isn't a fit

**The brand no longer exists.** **Kasa acquired Mint House on 2026-01-14**, and **minthouse.com now
301-redirects to minthouse.kasa.com.** You cannot book a Mint House because there is no Mint House.

**And the acquisition doesn't help us, because the surviving properties are all elsewhere.** Of the
properties that transitioned to Kasa, **all nine are outside Washington.** Nothing from the Mint
House portfolio landed on the Eastside.

**The Seattle site is closed and has been rebranded twice over.** The former location at **104 Pine
St is CLOSED** and now operates as **"Reside Seattle, a Wyndham Residence"** — a different company
entirely. Chasing the Mint House Seattle address leads to a Wyndham property, not a Kasa one.

**Even at its peak, Mint House never had Eastside inventory.** Downtown Seattle at ~25 minutes was
already outside the 20-minute Redmond rule. So this was never an option — the shutdown just made it
unambiguous.

**Where it actually leads:** if the Mint House product appealed, the live successor is
**[Kasa](./Kasa_Living.mdx)** — which has exactly **one** Eastside unit, a 2BR in downtown Bellevue,
and its own budget and no-phone problems.
