## Why this isn't a fit

**Zeus Living shut down in November 2023** after raising **$150M**. The company was backed by Airbnb
and still didn't survive. It has been gone for over two and a half years.

**Its landlords migrated to [Blueground](../blueground/Blueground.mdx)** — which is a genuinely useful
fact, not just trivia. If a Zeus-era listing looks appealing, the same unit may well be bookable
through Blueground today, under a company that still exists. Blueground is our known base option and
has 23+ Redmond units.

**Any Zeus listing still visible anywhere is stale.** Aggregators and SEO pages don't always clean up
after a shutdown, so the brand can still surface in a search.

**And this is the part to take seriously: any site presenting itself as Zeus Living today should be
treated as a SCAM SURFACE.** A dead brand with residual search traffic and no one left to defend the
trademark is exactly what fraudsters reuse. If Tom or Bryan lands on something calling itself Zeus
and asking for a deposit, that is not a discovery — it is a trap. **Never wire, Zelle, Venmo, or
gift-card money to it.**
