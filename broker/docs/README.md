## Key takeaway up front

Most "senior brokers" in our area are **senior living placement advisors**, not
classic apartment-rental brokers. Their service is usually **free to the family**
because the senior community pays them a referral fee. Washington is unusual: it
was the **first state in the nation to regulate** these agencies (RCW 18.330,
2011), so they must give you a **written fee-disclosure statement before** making
a referral. See the individual files for detail and sources.
