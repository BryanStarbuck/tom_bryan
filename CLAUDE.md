# Tom Starbuck

This directory holds work that Bryan Starbuck does to help his dad, **Tom Starbuck**.

## Purpose

This is a shared workspace for Bryan to help Tom with various things. Bryan sets up
charters and tasks here, works on them, and shares the results with Tom.

## People

* **Tom Starbuck** — Bryan's dad. The person this directory is here to help.
* **Bryan Starbuck** — Tom's son. Owner of this directory; does the work to help Tom.

## Charters

This directory tends to hold one charter at a time — a focused task to help Tom.

### Current charter: Find an apartment

Help Tom find an apartment in **Redmond, Washington**, or somewhere nearby within
about a **20-minute drive**.

## Subdirectories

* `apartment/` — Apartment search for Tom. Listings, options, and notes for finding a
  place in Redmond, WA (or within ~20 minutes).
* `broker/` — Finding a real estate / apartment broker who helps locate apartments,
  **especially brokers who specialize in helping seniors**. Goal: build a list of
  candidate brokers to contact.
